About Peridot

Peridot, with its vibrant lime-green hue, is a gemstone that radiates warmth and positivity. Known as the "Evening Emerald," peridot has been cherished for centuries for its distinctive color and clarity. This beautiful gemstone, a variety of the mineral olivine, symbolizes strength, prosperity, and renewal. Peridot's captivating green tones, reminiscent of a spring meadow, make it a favorite among gem enthusiasts and jewelry designers alike.

Peridot's striking green color and excellent transparency make it a popular choice for various types of jewelry. It is commonly used in rings, necklaces, earrings, and bracelets, where its vibrant hue can be fully appreciated. Peridot pairs beautifully with both yellow and white metals, such as gold and silver, and is often accented with diamonds or other complementary gemstones. Its affordability and distinctive appearance make peridot a versatile and attractive option for both everyday wear and special occasions.

At Karen Brand Limited, we source our peridot from the finest mines in Ethiopia. Our team ensures that each piece is ethically mined and meticulously inspected for quality. Whether you are a seasoned collector or new to the world of gemstones, our peridot collection offers something truly special for everyone.
