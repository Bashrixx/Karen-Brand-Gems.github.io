About the Owner

Karen Elizabeth, the visionary founder of Karen Brand Limited, embarked on her entrepreneurial journey as a sole proprietor. Her relentless dedication and unmatched work ethic propelled her to expand the business in 2011, incorporating it into a renowned entity, licensed by the Kenya Government Mininstry of Mining. Karen's global connections have significantly contributed to the company's success, making it a leader in the gemstone industry and still we are in the process of opening new branches in Singapore and in the United States of America. She continues to drive innovation and excellence, ensuring that Karen Brand Limited remains at the forefront of the local and international market.

About Karen Brand Limited Company

Since its inception in 2011, Karen Brand Limited Company has been dedicated to sourcing and supplying the highest quality precious and semi-precious gemstones, metals, minerals, and crystals. Our commitment to excellence and ethical practices has established us as a trusted name in the industry.

Our clientele includes esteemed buyers from United States of America, Singapore, China, Hong Kong, Thailand, Dubai, and beyond. With a dedicated team of 11 permanently employed professionals, Karen Brand Limited Company continues to innovate and lead, providing exceptional products and services to our global customers.

We pride ourselves on our meticulous inspection processes, ensuring that each gemstone meets our stringent quality standards. Our expert team uses both advanced technology and traditional methods to authenticate and inspect every gem, guaranteeing our customers receive only the best.

We are also a licensed company; Licensed by the Kenya Government Ministry of Mining.
