About Ruby

Ruby, with its vivid red hue, is a gemstone that exudes passion, power, and allure. Known as the "King of Gems," ruby has been cherished for centuries as a symbol of love, vitality, and nobility. This precious gemstone, a variety of the mineral corundum, is celebrated for its exceptional color, durability, and rarity. Rubies' intense red tones, often likened to the color of a glowing flame, make them one of the most sought-after gemstones in the world.

Ruby's captivating color and exceptional hardness make it a premier choice for fine jewelry. It is frequently used in rings, necklaces, earrings, and bracelets, where its vibrant red hues can be showcased. Rubies pair beautifully with various metals, particularly gold and platinum, and are often accented with diamonds or other complementary gemstones. Ruby's timeless elegance and rarity make it a favored choice for engagement rings and heirloom pieces.

At Karen Brand Limited, we source our ruby from the finest mines in Kenya and Burma. Our team ensures that each piece is ethically mined and meticulously inspected for quality. Whether you are a seasoned collector or new to the world of gemstones, our ruby collection offers something truly special for everyone.
