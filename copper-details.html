About Copper

Copper, a versatile and essential metal, has played a crucial role in human history and continues to be highly valued today. Known for its distinctive reddish-brown color and excellent conductivity, copper is used in a wide array of applications, from electrical wiring to jewelry. Its natural beauty and unique properties make copper a standout material, appreciated both for its practicality and aesthetic appeal.

Copper's exceptional conductivity makes it indispensable in the electrical industry, used extensively in wiring, motors, and generators. It is also a key component in plumbing, roofing, and industrial machinery. Beyond its practical uses, copper is a popular material in art and jewelry. Its warm color and malleability make it ideal for creating beautiful and intricate designs. Over time, copper develops a natural patina, adding to its character and appeal.

At Karen Brand Limited, we source our copper from the finest mines in DRCongo. Our team ensures that each piece is ethically mined and meticulously inspected for quality. Whether you are a seasoned collector or new to the world of minerals, our copper collection offers something truly special for everyone.
