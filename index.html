Why Choose Us

At Karen Brand Gems, we are committed to providing ethically sourced gemstones of the highest quality. Our team of experts ensures that each gem is handpicked and carefully inspected to meet our rigorous standards. We are also licensed by the Kenya Government Ministry of Mining.

Our Mining Operations

We currently source our gemstones from mines in Kenya, Tanzania, Ethiopia, Rwanda, Burundi, Madagascar, and DRC Congo. Our mining operations are designed to be efficient and environmentally friendly, ensuring the best quality gemstones.

Our Packaging Operations

We prioritize responsible gemstone packaging practices, focusing on sustainability and safety of our gemstones. We ensure that our packaging operations have minimal impact on the environment and support the local communities.
