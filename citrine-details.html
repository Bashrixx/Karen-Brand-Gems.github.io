About Citrine

Citrine, with its warm, golden hues, is a gemstone that exudes positivity and joy. Known as the "Merchant's Stone" for its supposed ability to attract wealth and prosperity, citrine has been cherished throughout history. This radiant gemstone ranges in color from pale yellow to deep amber, resembling the sun's vibrant energy. Citrine is a variety of quartz and is highly valued for its beauty and metaphysical properties.

Citrine's captivating color and excellent durability make it a favorite in the world of jewelry. It is often used in rings, necklaces, earrings, and bracelets, adding a touch of sunshine to any piece. Citrine pairs beautifully with various metals, including gold and silver, and complements other gemstones, making it a versatile choice for both classic and contemporary designs.

At Karen Brand Limited, we source our citrine from the finest mines in Madagascar. Our team ensures that each piece is ethically mined and meticulously inspected for quality. Whether you are a seasoned collector or new to the world of gemstones, our citrine collection offers something truly special for everyone.
