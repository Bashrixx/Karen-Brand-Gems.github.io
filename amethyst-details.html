About Amethyst

Amethyst, a variety of quartz, is renowned for its striking purple hue, ranging from light lavender to deep violet. This beautiful gemstone has been treasured throughout history for its stunning color and alleged mystical properties. Ancient Greeks believed that Amethyst could protect them from drunkenness and promote sobriety.

Amethyst is often associated with qualities such as calmness, clarity, and spiritual growth. It is commonly used in jewelry, such as rings, necklaces, and earrings, and is also a popular choice for decorative pieces and amulets. The gemstone's unique color and vibrant energy make it a favorite among collectors and enthusiasts.

At Karen Brand Gems, we source our amethyst from the finest mines in Rwanda and DRCongo. Our team ensures that each piece is ethically mined and meticulously inspected for quality. Whether you are a seasoned collector or new to the world of gemstones, our amethyst collection offers something truly special for everyone.
