About Tsavorite

Tsavorite is a rare and dazzling green gemstone that is celebrated for its vibrant color and exceptional brilliance. Known for its striking green hues and superb clarity, tsavorite is a member of the garnet family and is prized for its rarity and intense, vivid green shades. Its captivating color and brilliance make it a coveted choice for both collectors and jewelry enthusiasts. Tsavorite embodies the essence of nature's lush greenery, symbolizing renewal, growth, and prosperity.

Tsavorite's stunning green color and brilliance make it a popular choice for fine jewelry. It is frequently used in rings, necklaces, earrings, and bracelets, where its vibrant hues can be showcased. Tsavorite pairs beautifully with various metals, including gold and platinum, and is often accented with diamonds or other complementary gemstones. Its rarity and captivating appearance make tsavorite a favored choice for high-end and bespoke jewelry designs.

At Karen Brand Limited, we source our Tsavorite from the finest mines in Kenya and Tanzania. Our team ensures that each piece is ethically mined and meticulously inspected for quality.
